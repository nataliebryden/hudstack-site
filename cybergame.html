Interactive learning

Learn cybersecurity by playing.

What it is

Strategy meets security awareness

An interactive cybersecurity strategy game and card deck that teaches real security concepts through gameplay. Perfect for those who love strategy games. Educational and genuinely fun.

Players face realistic cyber scenarios including phishing attempts, social engineering, data breaches, and password challenges. Make decisions, face consequences, and learn from mistakes in a safe, engaging environment.

"No technical knowledge required. Just sharp thinking and healthy suspicion."

Gameplay

How It Works

Face real scenarios

Players encounter realistic cyber situations drawn from the threats organisations and individuals face every day.

Make decisions

Choose how to respond. Every decision has consequences, just like in the real world.

Learn and improve

The debrief after each round reinforces the lessons and builds lasting awareness.

Designed for teams, families, classrooms, and corporate training sessions.

Strategy Game
