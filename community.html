Why it matters

Everyone deserves to feel safe online

Senior citizens are disproportionately targeted by scammers. Phone scams, phishing emails, and fake websites are increasing, and many people have never been shown how to spot them.

Our programs change that. We empower people with the knowledge and confidence to protect themselves. No jargon. No judgement. Just clear, practical guidance in a welcoming environment.

Topics covered

What We Cover

  • Recognising phone and email scams
  • Safe online banking
  • Email safety and phishing awareness
  • Social media privacy and settings
  • How to spot fake websites
  • What to do if you think you've been scammed
  • Staying safe when shopping online

How we teach

Our Approach

We go at your pace. Beginner-friendly, patient explanations with plenty of time for questions. We use storytelling to make lessons memorable and entertaining, drawing on real examples that feel familiar and relevant to everyday life.

We use live demonstrations and printed take-home guides so participants leave with something they can refer back to. No one gets left behind, and no question is too basic.
