What it is

Hands-on. High-stakes. Unforgettable.

An immersive, hands-on team experience where participants solve puzzles based on real cybersecurity scenarios. Decode encrypted messages, identify phishing emails, crack passwords, respond to a simulated data breach, and race the clock.

Available in person or online, our escape rooms are designed so that no technical knowledge is required. Just sharp thinking, good teamwork, and a healthy dose of suspicion.
