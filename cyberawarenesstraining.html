Why it matters

Human behaviour is where breaches begin

90% of cyber breaches start with a human action. A clicked phishing link, a weak password, an overshared piece of information. Technology alone cannot protect your organisation.

Your people need to know what to look for. Our training workshops give them that knowledge in a way that sticks. Engaging, practical, and tailored to your industry and team.

What We Cover

Topics are modular. We tailor each workshop to the needs of your team and industry.

Phishing & Email Security

Recognising malicious emails, links, and attachments

Password Security & MFA

Creating strong passwords and using multi-factor authentication

Social Engineering

Recognising manipulation tactics by phone, email, and in person

Data Protection & Privacy

Handling sensitive information and privacy obligations

Mobile Device Security

Remote Work Security

Physical Security

Clean desk, secure devices, visitor awareness
